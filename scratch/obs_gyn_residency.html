    The OBS-GYN Residency Program is a comprehensive course designed specifically for MS OBS-GYN students in their residency years. Covering all essential topics required for theory and practical exams, this program offers a holistic approach to OBS-GYN exam preparations.

    The course is divided into four major parts, including basic sciences as related to Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Obstetrics including diseases of Neonates, Principles and Practice of Gynaecology and Gynaecological Pathology and Recent Advances in Obstetrics & Gynaecology. With a duration of approximately 200+ hours, it provides an in-depth knowledge of the key aspects of OBS-GYN.

    This course serves as a one-stop solution for MS OBS-GYN residents, offering curated content for exam preparation. With a busy schedule, it saves time by consolidating information from various sources into comprehensive lectures. Our dedicated faculty has meticulously compiled all essential information in a manner that fosters concept-building and improves clinical approach.

    Whether you're a 1st-year, 2nd-year, or 3rd-year residency, this course is tailored to cover the entire three-year residency syllabus. Additionally, it can be utilized for creating content for journal clubs and seminars.
